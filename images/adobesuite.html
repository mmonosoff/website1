Hooded seals are an endangered animal that primarily live in the artic and are distinguishable by their brightly colored "hood" that protrudes from their noses. In this poster there is a limited color pallette of only 7 colors, any variance is done simply through stripped pattering. I thought this was pretty cool : ).

In a 10 second animation using the same restriction of 7 colors and patterns, show hooded seals in their natural habitat and behaviours. Also make all the sounds effects to accompany the animation.
